Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below
— but here's a brief overview of what you can expect
when you make this top-rated sweet potato casserole:

Steps

  1. Cook and mash the sweet potatoes.
  2. Mix the casserole ingredients and transfer to a baking dish
  3. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the sweet potato mixture
  4. Bake until the topping is lightly browned.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
